Load Central by Kent Carumba

LoadCentral is a distribution solution provider for all prepaid services such as E-Loads, Physical Call Cards, Prepaid Internet Cards, Online Gaming Cards, Satellite Cards and others in the Philippines.

This project is my own version of the LoadCentral Retailer App. Through this app, retailers can sell prepaid airtime credits and can also sell popular prepaid card products (e-load & Aload, prepaid call cards, prepaid internet cards, online gaming cards, etc.) without having any sort of physical cards in their possession. Futhermore, retailers can do Balance Inquiry, Send Bank Deposit Details, View List of Authorized Bank Accounts for depositing purposes. The most notable feature of this app is the Contact list where retailers can conveniently browse through their contact list to reload their clients.

Roles: Android and iOS Developer

Technologies: Native Android, Java, Swift, React-Native

Load Central Dealer App by Kent Carumba

Currently, LoadCentral Philippines doesn't have an official dealer app thus, this project was made. Through this app, dealers can register retailers and replenish load to their retailers. Futhermore, dealers can do Balance Inquiry, Send Bank Deposit Details, View List of Authorized Bank Accounts for depositing purposes and also resend load through LoadCentral Access Numbers.

Roles: Android Developer

Technologies: Native Android, Java

W&L Aged Care Funding Instrument (ACFI) Calculator

Wellness & Lifestyles (W&L) is an aged care services provider based in Australia. This project is about the creation as well as the maintenance and bugfixes of W&L's ACFI Calculator App in Android and iOS.

W&L’s Aged Care Funding Instrument (ACFI) Calculator, both Android and iOS apps, will allow the client to quickly and easily calculate a resident’s ACFI rate based on their current care needs and compare it to what they are currently receiving.

Roles: Freelance Android and iOS Developer

Technologies: Native Android, Java, Objective-C, Swift
